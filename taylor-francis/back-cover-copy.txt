# Back Cover Copy

(1) An introductory paragraph to interest the non-specialist, the general
    reader, the librarian or bookseller (about 50 words).



(2) A second factual and informative paragraph which expands on the first by
    describing the main item or areas covered by the book (at least 100 words)

 

(3) A paragraph which illustrates the main findings and originality of the
    research, or specifies its particular usefulness to its intended audience
    (about 50 words).
